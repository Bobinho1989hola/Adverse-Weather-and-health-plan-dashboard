## Adverse Weather and Health Plan (AWHP) dashboard ##

### Project Overview

- The goal of the project is to track the progress of the AWHP against its objectives. The main objective is to protect public health in the event of adverse weather events. The key metrics and KPIs are summarised on the AWHP dashboard

<br>

---

#### Business Objecitves:
- To protect public health in the event of adverse weather events
- To support healthcare professionals to plan, prepare and respond to adverse weather events

<br>

---

#### Tools
- Power Bi
- Power Query
- Dax
- Excel
- Python

<br>

---

#### Key Metrics
- Number of registrants to seasonal webinar events
- Weather Health Alerts registrations
- Years of life lost due to adverse weather events
- Amount of hospitalisations due to adverse weather events

<br>

---


#### Insights
- Since the inception of the AWHP, the number of registrations to receive the Weather Health Alerts is growing
- Since the inception of the AWHP, the amount of hospitalisations is decreasing year on year
- Since the inception of the AWHP, the amount of years of life lost is decreasing year on year
